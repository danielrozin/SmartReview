## Prioritization Framework

### Tier 1: High-DA Authority Sites (DA 70+) — 25 prospects
Long-term relationship building. Pitch data partnerships and expert contributor roles. Expect 2-4 week response times. One quality backlink from these = dozens from lower-DA sites.

### Tier 2: Mid-DA Review/Niche Sites (DA 35-69) — 45 prospects
Primary outreach targets. Guest posts, comparison data sharing, content syndication. Faster turnaround, more likely to accept contributions. Aim for 5-10 placements/month.

### Tier 3: Lower-DA Niche Blogs (DA <35) — 28 prospects
Quick wins for link volume. Write-for-us programs, guest posts with minimal friction. Can batch outreach. Aim for 10-15 placements/month.

### Outreach Cadence
- **Week 1:** Personalize and send Tier 2 pitches (highest ROI)
- **Week 2:** Follow up on Tier 2 + begin Tier 3 batch outreach
- **Week 3:** Tier 1 relationship-building pitches (higher touch)
- **Week 4:** Follow-up all + track results

### Success Metrics
- Response rate target: 15-20% (industry avg for targeted outreach)
- Placement rate target: 5-8% of pitches
- Monthly link target: 15-20 new referring domains
- Priority metric: links from DA 40+ sites in mattress/coffee verticals (highest keyword alignment)
