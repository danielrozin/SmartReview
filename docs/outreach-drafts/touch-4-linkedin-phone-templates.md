# Touch 4 — LinkedIn/Phone Direct Outreach Templates (Day 10)

> Final touch in the 4-touch brand outreach sequence.
> Use when Touches 1-3 have been sent with no response, OR as an accelerator alongside Touch 1 for Tier A brands.

---

## LinkedIn Connection Request Template

**Note:** Customize for each target's partnership/marketing director. Keep under 300 characters.

```
Hi [NAME] — I head partnerships at SmartReview.com. We see [BRAND] in
[XX,000+] monthly "[brand] vs" searches and have a sponsorship model
that lets you own that comparison narrative. Worth a quick chat?
```

## Phone Call Script (Live Conversation Version)

```
Hi [NAME], this is [YOUR NAME] from SmartReview. Do you have a quick
moment?

[If yes:]
Great — SmartReview is a product comparison platform, and we've noticed
that [BRAND] comes up in over [XX,000] monthly "vs" searches on Google.
People are actively comparing [BRAND] to [COMPETITOR] right now.

We offer sponsored placements on these comparison pages — so instead
of a competitor's message showing up when someone searches "[BRAND] vs
[COMPETITOR]," your brand's messaging is front and center.

We have three tiers starting at $500 a month, and we share weekly
click-through and engagement data so you can see exactly what you're
getting.

Would it make sense to schedule a 15-minute demo where I can show you
a sample page with [BRAND]'s placement?

[If they ask about pricing:]
Our tiers are $500 for basic sponsored placement, $1,000 for premium
with a dedicated landing section, and $2,000 for exclusive category
ownership. Each tier includes weekly analytics reporting.

[If they want to think about it:]
Totally understand. Can I send you a one-pager with a sample mockup
and our pricing tiers? What email works best for you?

[If no time:]
No problem at all. Can I send you a quick email with a one-pager?
What's the best email to reach you at?
```

## Tier A Brand-Specific LinkedIn Notes

### Apple (AirPods)
- **Target role:** Partnerships Manager or Affiliate Marketing, Apple Services
- **Hook:** "50,000+ monthly 'AirPods vs' searches — your brand dominates this space, let's make sure your message does too"
- **Note:** Apple rarely does direct sponsorships at this scale. Position as data partnership or content licensing instead.

### Sony (WF-1000XM5)
- **Target role:** Digital Marketing Manager, Sony Electronics NA
- **Hook:** "20,000+ 'Sony vs AirPods' searches/mo — comparison shoppers are your highest-intent audience"

### iRobot (Roomba)
- **Target role:** Brand Partnerships or Digital Marketing, iRobot
- **Hook:** "18,000+ 'Roomba vs' searches/mo — we can help you win the comparison conversation"
- **Note:** iRobot under Amazon ownership may route through Amazon Ads partnerships.

### Nespresso
- **Target role:** Digital Marketing or Partnerships, Nespresso US
- **Hook:** "15,000+ 'Nespresso vs' searches/mo — coffee buyers are comparing before they buy"

### Ninja (SharkNinja)
- **Target role:** Digital Partnerships or Brand Marketing, SharkNinja
- **Hook:** "21,000+ monthly searches across air fryers and coffee — your cross-category presence is unique"
- **Note:** Ninja/Shark are the same company — bundle pitch across both product lines.
